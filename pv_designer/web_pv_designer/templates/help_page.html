Locate Your Address

Begin by using the search bar to find your address. Zoom into the map to precisely locate the area where you envision installing the solar panels.

Mark the Installation Area

Select the 'Add New Area' option and draw on the map to outline where you wish to place the solar panels. This area represents your future solar installation.

Populate the Area with Panels

Click on 'Fill Area with Panels'. A red edge indicates the top of the roof. Should the orientation need adjusting, utilize the rotate button to align the panels properly.

Specify Panel Details

Now, select your PV panel type from the available options, fill in the slope value, and choose the panel's orientation to ensure optimal solar gain.

Adjust Panel Placement

Should you need to reposition or remove a panel, simply click on the panel in question. Use the provided buttons to either move it to a new location or delete it entirely.
